About Toffee Bar

Green Tree House looking Restarant Dining n Cafe located at Premium Location in South Delhi- Chanakya Puri , Opp. Hotel Taj Palace Entry Gate, ranging approx. 1 km from Durga Bhai Deshmukh Metro Station South Campus, serving premium Food n Beverages.

Toffee Bar in Chanakya Puri is a beautiful venue of all budgets, families, cultures, traditions, and communities. Here you can host all your functions from pre-wedding to the birthday party with much zeal and fervor. Also, you will appreciate the service provided by the experienced and friendly staff. You won’t find this venue lacking in anything from space to decoration and furnishings to lighting.

The Toffee Bar has a multi-cuisine restaurant to serve both vegetarian and non-vegetarian delights. Here serving delicacies is a tradition instead of a service. Just like everything has a theme, you can also give your menu a theme like Indian, Chinese, and continental.

Invite your guests to a sumptuous dinner prepared and served by the seasoned hands at Toffee Bar.
